# 📍 Kolkata Katha — AI-Powered Heritage Crowdsourcing Platform

Kolkata Katha is a community-driven archive that preserves the *hidden heritage* of Kolkata.  
It allows everyday people to contribute photos, memories, and stories about local spots — chai stalls, trams, para addas, old rajbaris — places often overlooked by mainstream tourism.  

Contributions are curated into expressive archival narratives using an LLM, creating a living museum of the city’s cultural texture.

---

Its a Brand new idea with complete end to end ownership of application with the author.

## ✨ Features

- **Interactive Map:** Explore Kolkata through pins marking heritage spots.  
- **Community Contributions:** Upload photos, write memories, and share stories tied to specific locations.  
- **AI Story Curation:** Raw memories are transformed into warm, archival narratives using Groq’s LLM API.  
- **Rich UI Design:** Distinctive Kolkata-inspired visuals — terracotta accents, gold glows, paper-grain textures, serif typography.  
- **Database Persistence:** SQLite backend with safe migrations for authors, images, and raw text.  
- **Story Cards:** Layered, animated cards with parallax and glow effects for immersive storytelling.  

---

## 🏆 Benefits

- **Preserves everyday heritage:** Captures stories of places beyond tourist landmarks — chai stalls, trams, para addas.  
- **Community ownership:** Anyone can contribute, making heritage preservation participatory instead of top-down.  
- **Cultural specificity:** Rejects generic SaaS aesthetics in favor of Kolkata’s textures, typography, and atmosphere.  
- **Accessible storytelling:** AI curation elevates raw memories into archival narratives while keeping the contributor’s voice.  
- **Scalable archive:** Stories are stored in a structured database, enabling aggregation and future expansion.  

---

## ⚠️ Problems It Solves

- **Local Heritage invisibility:** Many local spots and traditions are undocumented and risk being forgotten.  
- **Fragmented memory:** Personal stories often remain private; this platform centralizes them into a shared archive.  
- **Generic design traps:** Most AI projects look sterile and globalized; Kolkata Katha insists on cultural authenticity.  
- **Barrier to contribution:** Not everyone can write polished stories — AI curation lowers the barrier while preserving intent.  
- **Data fragility:** Safe migrations ensure contributors’ data isn’t lost when schema evolves.  

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Frontend/UI:** [Streamlit](https://streamlit.io/) with custom CSS and Kolkata-inspired design language.  
- **Mapping:** [Folium](https://python-visualization.github.io/folium/) + [streamlit-folium](https://github.com/randyzwitch/streamlit-folium).  
- **Database:** SQLite with safe migrations for authors, images, and raw text.  
- **AI Curation:** Groq LLM API (`llama-3.1-8b-instant`).  
- **Deployment:** Streamlit Cloud.  


Kolkata Katha is not just an app — it’s a living museum.
By blending community contributions with AI curation, it ensures that the texture of everyday Kolkata — para gossip, tram rides, chai stalls — is preserved for future generations.
